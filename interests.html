Gaming Preferences

Gaming has always been a part of my life, almost as much as reading.

  • UNDERTALE/DELTARUNE - These games changed everything for me. They built my way of seeing games and storys. UNDERTALE even had it's 10th Anniverary a couple days ago.
  • Psychological Horror RPGs - for some reason, game's like that are comforting to me. For Example: Project Kat and Paper Lily, I really love those.
  • NO CHEAP JUMPSCARES! - I jump easily and honestly? It just pisses me off.
  • Top-Down RPG Style - There's something perfect about the classic overhead games that i really like a lot, like earthbound and rpgmaker games.
  • Indie Games - Indie games really give me the atmosphere that i like the most.
